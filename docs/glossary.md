# Glossary

**Co-op / cooperative**: a member-governed organization where users are also owners.

**Resource co-op**: a co-op that manages scarce capacity (energy, bandwidth, compute, messaging, etc.) with explicit fairness + congestion rules.

**Membership (co-op)**: a non-transferable credential granting voice/entitlements, typically backed by dues and/or a bond.

**Sybil attack**: one actor presenting as many accounts to steal voice/subsidies.

**Quadratic voting/funding**: mechanisms that reduce the influence of pure wealth and favor broad participation.

**Fork-and-merge governance**: governance that treats divergence as normal (fork), with structured ways to reconcile (merge).

**Exit ramp**: the ability to export state and leave without losing your history or being trapped.
